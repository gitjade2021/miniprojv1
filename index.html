Laoag City

The capital of the Philippine province of Ilocos Norte, Laoag is formally known as the City of Laoag
(Ilocano: Siudad ti Laoag; Filipino: Lungsod ng Laoag). It is a third-class component city.
It is home to the busiest commercial airport in the Ilocos Region and is the
largest populous, political, commercial, and industrial center of the province.
